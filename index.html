Chicken Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • Bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, cumin (just a pinch of each)
  • Salt

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, lemon juice, and salt. Marinate for 30 mins to 1 hour.
  2. Boil rice in water with bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, cumin, and salt for 6-7 mins (don't fully cook it). Drain.
  3. Heat ghee/oil. Fry onions until golden, set some aside for garnish. Add ginger-garlic paste, then chopped tomatoes. Cook for a few minutes. Add marinated chicken, cook for 10-12 mins until done
  4. In a pot, layer rice and chicken gravy. Add saffron (optional) and drizzle ghee. Garnish with fried onions.
  5. Cover the pot and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Fluff the biryani and serve with raita or salad.

Mutton Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp chili powder, 1/2 tbsp turmeric, 1 tbsp garam masala
  • Salt & 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Chopped mint & coriander
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Bay leaf, cloves, cardamoms, cinnamon, salt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup ghee/oil

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Mix mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, salt, lemon juice, and herbs. Marinate for 1-2 hours.
  2. Boil rice with spices for 5-7 mins (half-cooked). Drain.
  3. Fry onions in ghee/oil, then add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes. Add marinated mutton, cook until tender.
  4. Layer mutton and rice in a pot. Sprinkle garam masala and herbs
  5. Cover and cook on low for 20-30 mins.
  6. Fluff and serve with raita.

Chicken Chukka

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add fennel seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes, green chilies, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add a little water (just enough to cover the chicken), cover, and cook until chicken is tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  7. Once the chicken is cooked, increase the heat to dry out excess water and allow the chicken to absorb the spices.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for 2 more minutes.
  9. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot

Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, and salt for 30 mins.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan until done (8-10 mins). Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt butter, sauté onion until golden.
  4. Add pureed tomato, chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Cook for 5 mins.
  5. Add cooked chicken, then stir in cream. Simmer for 5 mins.
  6. Serve with naan or rice

Mutton Paya

Ingredients

  • 4 mutton trotters
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 tomato (chopped)
  • Fresh coriander
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Clean trotters and wash them well.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, sauté chopped onion until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1-2 mins.
  4. Add tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Cook until soft.
  5. Add mutton trotters and water (enough to cover them). Bring to a boil.
  6. Simmer on low heat for 2-3 hours until the trotters are soft and the broth thickens.
  7. Add garam masala, cook for 5 more mins.
  8. Garnish with coriander and serve hot

Panner Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (pureed)
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan, sauté chopped onion until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1-2 mins.
  3. Add tomato puree, chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5 mins.
  4. Add paneer cubes and mix gently.
  5. Stir in cream and cook for 5 mins.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for 2 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Vegetable Pulao

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and set aside
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and sauté chopped onion until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1-2 mins.
  4. Add tomato and mixed vegetables, cook for 3-4 mins.
  5. Add rice and mix well. Add 2 cups water and salt.
  6. Cook on low heat for 10-15 mins until rice is done.
  7. Add paneer cubes and garam masala, mix gently.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Aloo Gobi

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes (peeled & chopped)
  • 1 small cauliflower (cut into florets)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 mins.
  3. Add tomato, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 3-4 mins.
  4. Add potatoes and cauliflower. Mix well.
  5. Add 1 cup water, cover, and cook for 15-20 mins until veggies are soft.
  6. Add garam masala and mix.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander.

Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 200g mushrooms (button or cremini), sliced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth, kept warm
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine (optional)
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Add the garlic and sliced mushrooms, and cook for 5-7 minutes until the mushrooms release their moisture and become tender.
  3. Add the Arborio rice and cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring constantly to toast the rice lightly.
  4. Pour in the white wine (if using) and cook until absorbed.
  5. Begin adding the warm vegetable broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Wait for the liquid to be absorbed before adding more broth. Continue until the rice is creamy and cooked through (about 20-25 minutes).
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, if using, and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.

Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1/4 cup cream or milk (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook onions & garlic: In a pot, heat butter/oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Cook until soft (3-4 mins).
  2. Add tomatoes: Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes until they soften.
  3. Add broth & seasonings: Pour in broth, salt, pepper, and sugar. Stir well and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Blend soup: Use an immersion blender (or regular blender) to blend until smooth.
  5. Add cream: Stir in cream or milk for a creamy texture (optional).
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh herbs and enjoy!

Sweet Corn Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sweet corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1 small potato (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tbsp corn flour (cornstarch) mixed with 2 tbsp water (for thickening)
  • 1 tbsp cream (optional)
  • Chopped spring onions or parsley (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cook onion & potato: Heat butter/oil in a pot. Add chopped onion and cook until soft (2-3 mins). Add chopped potato and cook for another 2 mins.
  2. Add corn & broth: Add sweet corn and broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 10-12 minutes until the potato is soft.
  3. Blend soup: Use a blender to puree the soup until smooth (optional, or keep it chunky).
  4. Add the corn flour slurry (cornstarch mixed with water) to the soup and stir. Cook for 2-3 minutes until it thickens.
  5. Add salt, pepper, and sugar (optional). Stir in cream for a richer taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions or parsley. Enjoy!

Minestrone Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • 1 celery stalk (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (14 oz)
  • 1 cup green beans (chopped)
  • 1 small potato (chopped)
  • 1 can white beans (15 oz)
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1 cup small pasta
  • Spinach (optional)
  • Parmesan (optional)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil, cook onion, carrot, and celery for 5 mins.
  2. Add garlic, tomatoes, green beans, potato, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour in broth, add beans, and simmer for 10-15 mins.
  4. Add pasta, cook 8-10 mins.
  5. Stir in spinach (optional).
  6. Serve with Parmesan (optional).

Hot and Sour Soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup bamboo shoots (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili paste or hot sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp corn flour (cornstarch) mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Spring onions (for garnish)

    Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Bring broth to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add mushrooms, tofu, and bamboo shoots. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, rice vinegar, chili paste, ginger, and garlic.
  4. Mix corn flour with water, then add to the soup to thicken
  5. Slowly pour in beaten egg, stirring gently to form ribbons.
  6. Season with white and black pepper.
  7. Garnish with spring onions.
